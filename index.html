The Colour of Love

With the ever-evolving dating scene in the 2020’s, it’s getting more and more difficult to find a partner that might be the one. Using the data collected in the How Couples Meet and Stay Together 2017 study by Stanford researchers, we hope our visualization helps you understand some of the factors that could affect important aspects of a relationship including its overall quality! Hover and click through our visualizations to dive deeper and explore interesting dependencies between age, sex habits, religiousness, and race.
